Romance, Gifts, Heart Events, and Marriage

Romance pages should be careful before launch: candidate names, favorite gifts, schedules, and event triggers must be verified before they become guide claims.

Why this URL matters

Romance is one of the highest-intent search categories for farming sims. Players want candidate lists, gift shortcuts, birthdays, rival or exclusivity rules, heart events, marriage requirements, and whether decisions can be reversed.

Content quality rules

Do not scrape official character art. Use original screenshots only where allowed, original diagrams, or text-first tables. Mark every gift table with the game version and last verification date because life-sim patches can change balance or requirements.
