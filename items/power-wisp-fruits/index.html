Power Wisp Fruits in Echoes of Teradea

Power Wisp Fruits are rewards from Power Statue challenges. Players trade them at the Forest Goddess Statue to increase stamina and unlock useful abilities. Current confirmed data, player implications, and remaining verification gaps.

Current answer

Power Wisp Fruits are rewards from Power Statue challenges. Players trade them at the Forest Goddess Statue to increase stamina and unlock useful abilities.

Confirmed data

  • Power Statues contain quick challenges or puzzles.
  • Completing them frees dormant Power Wisps.
  • The resulting fruits support permanent progression.

What this means for players

Power Wisp Fruits connect exploration, puzzle completion, stamina growth, and ability unlocks. They should eventually be tracked by statue location, reward amount, upgrade cost, and remaining total.

Still awaiting verification

Fruit totals, per-statue rewards, exchange costs, ability list, and upgrade caps remain unknown.
