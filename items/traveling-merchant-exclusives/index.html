Traveling Merchant Exclusive Items

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that Natsume says cannot be found anywhere else. Current confirmed data, player implications, and remaining verification gaps.

Current answer

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that Natsume says cannot be found anywhere else.

Confirmed data

  • Merchants are tied to campsites.
  • Their inventory includes exclusive items.
  • No merchant names or products have been revealed.

What this means for players

These items will need a merchant-by-merchant database with campsite, appearance conditions, schedule, price, purchase limit, restock behavior, and downstream use.

Still awaiting verification

Merchant identities, routes, schedules, item names, prices, limits, and restock rules remain unknown.
