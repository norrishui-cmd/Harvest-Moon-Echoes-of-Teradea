Mounts and Pets Guide

Reported feature lists mention mounts and pets, so this page focuses on what players need to know: unlocks, movement, care, bond rewards, and whether companions affect exploration or farming.

Questions this guide should answer

  • When mounts and pets unlock, and whether any are tied to restoration or story progress.
  • Which companions are cosmetic and which change movement, gathering, farming, or safety.
  • Care requirements, bond levels, food, brushing, housing, and whether neglect has consequences.
  • Region-specific movement advantages, including shortcuts or terrain access.

Launch table structure

Each companion entry should include type, unlock location, required cost or quest, care routine, special ability, best use case, and related regions. Avoid claiming abilities until tested in-game.

Internal link strategy

This URL should connect to the map guide for route value, the farming guide for care routines, and the item database for required food or equipment. That prevents the companion guide from becoming a thin list.
