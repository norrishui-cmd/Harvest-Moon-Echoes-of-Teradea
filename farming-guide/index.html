Systems Guide

Crops, Animals, Tools, and Farm Layouts

A high-quality farming page should help players compare profit, time, stamina, space, tool upgrades, and restoration needs instead of listing items without context.

Crop table requirements

The crop guide should not publish best-crop claims until seed prices, growth days, regrowth behavior, season limits, quality tiers, and selling prices are verified. The useful launch table should calculate profit per day and also flag crops needed for cooking, gifts, quests, or village restoration.

Animals and tools

Animal and tool coverage should answer practical questions: when animals unlock, how care affects output, whether products have quality tiers, which tools save the most stamina, and which upgrades are required for map or restoration progress.

Farm layout guidance

Once the farm map is known, include layouts for early money, low-maintenance animals, crafting material storage, and completionist production. Layout screenshots should be original captures or diagrams, not copied official artwork.
