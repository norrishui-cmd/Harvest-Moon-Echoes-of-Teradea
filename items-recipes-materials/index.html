Echoes of Teradea Items and Materials

Only named or clearly described items from current official material are indexed; unannounced crops, recipes, gifts, prices, and crafting materials remain outside the sitemap.

Confirmed item records

Nautical Charts in Echoes of Teradea

Nautical Charts are confirmed exploration items used to locate remote islands where players can hunt for treasure and befriend rare animals not found on the mainland.

Power Wisp Fruits in Echoes of Teradea

Power Wisp Fruits are rewards from Power Statue challenges. Players trade them at the Forest Goddess Statue to increase stamina and unlock useful abilities.

Ore and Gems in Echoes of Teradea

Ore and gems are confirmed resources found in maze-like caves across Teradea. Quarrytop is the named mining village, but the official material does not place every mining resource there.

Traveling Merchant Exclusive Items

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that Natsume says cannot be found anywhere else.

Rare Collectibles in Echoes of Teradea

Rare collectibles are confirmed across Teradea’s open world, including caves, hidden areas, and remote islands reached through exploration systems.

Current database boundary

The official material currently supports charts, Power Wisp Fruits, ore and gems, merchant exclusives, and rare collectibles. Individual crop, recipe, gift, tool, and price pages will remain unpublished until the game provides concrete records.
