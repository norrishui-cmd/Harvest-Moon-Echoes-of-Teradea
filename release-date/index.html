Harvest Moon: Echoes of Teradea Release Date and Platforms

Natsume has confirmed the September 24, 2026 launch date and all five platform families. This page separates those facts from release-time details still awaiting store listings.

Current status

Harvest Moon: Echoes of Teradea releases September 24, 2026 on Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S and PC via Steam. Natsume confirmed the date and platforms in its June 18, 2026 announcement.

For a guide site, this page is a high-value URL because players usually search release timing before they search detailed gameplay systems. It should be updated whenever official publisher pages, Nintendo eShop listings, trailers, or press releases add concrete details.

What to check before launch

  • Exact digital unlock time by region; Natsume has confirmed the date, not an hour-by-hour launch schedule.
  • Digital release time, preload status, file size, and language support once store pages are available.
  • Physical edition, digital edition, preorder bonuses, and any limited edition content if announced.
  • Whether save data, performance, or feature differences exist between Nintendo Switch and Switch 2.
  • Official age rating, publisher page, trailer links, screenshots, and final description.

Source policy

Because Echoes of Teradea is unreleased, this site uses a strict label system: confirmed for official publisher or platform information, reported for reputable media listings, and unverified for community claims that need checking after launch. Official source: Natsume's June 18 announcement.
