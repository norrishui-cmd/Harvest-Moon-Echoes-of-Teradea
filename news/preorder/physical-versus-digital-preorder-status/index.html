Physical and Digital Preorder Status Explained

Physical preorders are confirmed for Switch 2, Switch, and PS5, while Xbox Series X|S and Steam remain confirmed launch platforms without a physical preorder announcement.

What was announced

Physical preorders are confirmed for Switch 2, Switch, and PS5, while Xbox Series X|S and Steam remain confirmed launch platforms without a physical preorder announcement.

Confirmed facts

  • Physical preorder platforms are explicitly limited in the announcement.
  • Xbox and Steam are still launch platforms.
  • A digital preorder schedule is not supplied.

What it means for players

This distinction prevents the platform list from being misread as an edition list. Digital store availability may appear later.

This update narrows one specific player question using the publisher’s wording. It does not turn unreleased footage or marketing copy into invented routes, values, schedules, or performance claims.

What remains unconfirmed

Digital preorder dates, preload, digital bonuses, Xbox physical availability, and edition differences remain unknown.
