What a Complete Animal Companion Guide Will Need

What was announced

Natsume confirms that animal companions have different abilities for terrain, obstacles, hidden areas, and treasure discovery.

Confirmed facts

  • Companions do more than follow the player.
  • Rocks and fallen trees are confirmed obstacles.
  • Different animals have different abilities.

What it means for players

Individual animal guides should wait for verified names, locations, befriending conditions, traversal skills, and bonding rules. The announcement supports the system, not a full roster.

This update narrows one specific player question using the publisher’s wording. It does not turn unreleased footage or marketing copy into invented routes, values, schedules, or performance claims.

What remains unconfirmed

The complete animal list, exact abilities, taming requirements, and upgrade conditions remain unpublished.
