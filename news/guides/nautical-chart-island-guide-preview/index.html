Nautical Charts Will Lead Players to Remote Islands

Natsume says players obtain nautical charts to locate remote islands for treasure hunting and rare-animal discovery.

What was announced

Natsume says players obtain nautical charts to locate remote islands for treasure hunting and rare-animal discovery.

Confirmed facts

  • Remote islands are not limited to scenery.
  • Charts are part of island discovery.
  • Some animals do not appear on the mainland.

What it means for players

A launch-ready island guide should record how each chart is obtained, where it points, access requirements, treasures, animals, and return travel options.

This update narrows one specific player question using the publisher’s wording. It does not turn unreleased footage or marketing copy into invented routes, values, schedules, or performance claims.
