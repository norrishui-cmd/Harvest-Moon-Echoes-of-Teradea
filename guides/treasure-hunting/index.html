Echoes of Teradea Treasure Hunting Guide

Treasure hunting is confirmed in caves, hidden areas and remote islands. Nautical charts reveal islands, while animal abilities and expanded movement help players reach secrets. Confirmed details, player implications and the information still awaiting launch verification.

Quick answer

Treasure hunting is confirmed in caves, hidden areas and remote islands. Nautical charts reveal islands, while animal abilities and expanded movement help players reach secrets.

Confirmed facts

  • Remote islands contain treasure-hunting opportunities.
  • Maze-like caves hold ore, gems and secrets.
  • Animal companions can uncover treasures and hidden areas.
  • Jumping, ladders and vines provide additional access routes.

What this means for players

A complete treasure route cannot be published before the locations are verified. At launch, every treasure entry should record its region, nearest landmark, access requirement, companion or movement gate, contents and whether it is missable.

Still to verify

  • Exact treasure locations and rewards
  • Whether treasure respawns
  • Required charts, companions or abilities
  • Completion totals and achievements

Source policy

Checked July 18, 2026 against Natsume’s official March 11 title announcement and June 18 gameplay-feature announcement. Unknown routes, values and entity names are excluded instead of inferred from earlier games.
