Echoes of Teradea Traveling Merchants Guide

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that are not available elsewhere. Their names, routes, schedules and inventories have not yet been disclosed. Confirmed details, player implications and the information still awaiting launch verification.

Quick answer

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that are not available elsewhere. Their names, routes, schedules and inventories have not yet been disclosed.

Confirmed facts

  • Traveling merchants are tied to campsites.
  • They offer exclusive items.
  • Those items are not found through ordinary sources elsewhere.
  • Camp locations and merchant schedules remain unconfirmed.

What this means for players

The useful launch version will need a merchant directory organized by campsite, day or season, arrival conditions, inventory, prices and restock rules. Until then, this page answers where the merchants appear and why players should check them.

Still to verify

  • Merchant names and campsite routes
  • Days, seasons and arrival triggers
  • Exclusive inventory and prices
  • Restock frequency and purchase limits

Source policy

Checked July 18, 2026 against Natsume’s official March 11 title announcement and June 18 gameplay-feature announcement. Unknown routes, values and entity names are excluded instead of inferred from earlier games.
