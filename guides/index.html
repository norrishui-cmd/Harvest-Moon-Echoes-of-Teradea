Echoes of Teradea Player Guides

Fact-checked guides for treasure hunting, mining, merchants, stamina, rare animals, pets and mounts.

Browse confirmed coverage

Every page below has a direct answer supported by official material. Exact locations and values remain excluded until verified.

Echoes of Teradea Treasure Hunting Guide

Treasure hunting is confirmed in caves, hidden areas and remote islands. Nautical charts reveal islands, while animal abilities and expanded movement help players reach secrets.

Echoes of Teradea Mining Caves, Ore, and Gems

Natsume confirms maze-like caves containing ore and gems. Quarrytop is also a named mining village, but exact mine floors, ore tables and tool requirements are not yet public.

Echoes of Teradea Traveling Merchants Guide

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and sell exclusive items that are not available elsewhere. Their names, routes, schedules and inventories have not yet been disclosed.

Echoes of Teradea Stamina Recovery and Upgrades

Players can recover stamina by resting at campsites. Power Wisp Fruits can also be exchanged at the Forest Goddess Statue to boost stamina and unlock useful abilities.

Echoes of Teradea Rare Animals and Island Wildlife

Rare animals can be found on remote islands, including animals that do not appear on the mainland. Players use nautical charts to locate those islands and can befriend the animals there.

Echoes of Teradea Pets, Animals, and Mounts Explained

Pets, animals and mounts can accompany the player, and each companion or mount has special abilities that help explore uncharted territory. The official material does not yet provide a full category-by-category roster.
