Farming Overview

Echoes of Teradea Farming, Crops, and Animals

Confirmed farming activities in Echoes of Teradea and the crop, animal and economy data still awaiting gameplay verification.

Quick answer

Echoes of Teradea retains the series’ core farm loop: players tend a farm, raise animals and harvest crops between journeys across Teradea. Exact crops, growth times, animal products and sell prices have not yet been published.

Confirmed facts

  • A player farm is part of the confirmed game loop.
  • Crop growing and harvesting are confirmed.
  • Farm animals can be raised.
  • Farming takes place between exploration and village-helping journeys.
  • No verified crop-profit or animal-product table is available yet.

What players should know

This overview establishes what the game includes while keeping numerical databases out of search until they contain real answers. The future crop pages must include season, seed source, cost, growth days, regrowth, sell price and profit per day; animal pages need purchase source, care, products and unlocks.

Still to verify

  • Full crop and flower list
  • Season rules and growth times
  • Animal species, care and products
  • Farm upgrades, layouts and automation

Source policy

Checked July 18, 2026 against Natsume’s March 11 title announcement, June 18 trailer announcement and official store information where relevant. Unknown mechanics are excluded rather than inferred from earlier Harvest Moon games.
