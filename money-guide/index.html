Money Guide and Early Profit Routes

Money pages can become thin if they only say sell valuable items. This guide is structured around verified profit math, opportunity cost, and save-or-sell rules.

Profit route categories

  • Crop profit per day after seed cost.
  • Animal products after care time and purchase cost.
  • Forage and mining routes by travel time and stamina cost.
  • Cooking or crafting profit after ingredient opportunity cost.
  • Quest and restoration rewards that unlock better income.

Safe-to-sell policy

No item should be marked safe to sell until gift, recipe, crafting, restoration, and quest uses are checked. The item database should be the source of truth.

Launch-week testing plan

Test early routes by measuring money gained, stamina spent, time spent, unlock dependencies, and whether the route blocks important progression.
