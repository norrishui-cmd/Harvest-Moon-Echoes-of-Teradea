Festivals and Seasonal Events Guide

Farming sim players care about calendars because missed events can delay romance, recipes, rewards, and completion. This page prepares the calendar structure without inventing event names.

Calendar fields to collect

FieldWhy it matters
Date and seasonLets players plan crops, gifts, and travel.
Entry time and locationPrevents missed events due to shop-hour style rules.
RequirementsShows items, animals, relationships, or story gates.
RewardsConnects events to recipes, tools, cosmetics, money, or relationship gains.

Preparation checklist

  • Save event items before selling seasonal crops.
  • Track birthdays separately from festivals if the game includes both.
  • Note whether festivals pause crop growth, shops, animal care, or shipping.
  • Verify if romance scenes trigger during or after events.
