Does Every Retailer Include the Lupo Plush?

No. The official wording ties the Lupo plush to preordering through the Natsume Store, not to every listed retailer. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

No. The official wording ties the Lupo plush to preordering through the Natsume Store, not to every listed retailer.

Confirmed details

  • Other retailers are named as sellers.
  • No universal plush promise is stated.
  • Retailer pages control their own displayed offers.

What this means for players

Check the exact listing before purchase; do not assume Amazon, Target, Walmart, Best Buy, or GameStop includes the same bonus.
