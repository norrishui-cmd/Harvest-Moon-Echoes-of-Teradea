What Happens If a Wild Animal Catches You?

If a wild animal catches the player, Natsume says the player loses collected items and is sent back to the farm. See the confirmed details and the information still awaiting gameplay verification.

Quick answer

If a wild animal catches the player, Natsume says the player loses collected items and is sent back to the farm.

Confirmed facts

  • Wolves, bears and tigers are confirmed threats.
  • Players are expected to evade or escape.
  • Collected-item loss is the stated penalty.
  • The player returns to the farm.

What players should know

This answer is limited to information published by Natsume. Exact values, triggers and platform behavior will be added only when official store pages or verified gameplay provide evidence.
