How Do You Recover Stamina in Echoes of Teradea?

Resting at campsites recovers stamina. Players can also cook meals at campfires, although exact food recovery values are not yet known. Confirmed details, player implications and the information still awaiting launch verification.

Quick answer

Resting at campsites recovers stamina. Players can also cook meals at campfires, although exact food recovery values are not yet known.

Confirmed facts

  • Campsites provide rest and stamina recovery.
  • Campfires support cooking.
  • Power Wisp progression can raise stamina.
