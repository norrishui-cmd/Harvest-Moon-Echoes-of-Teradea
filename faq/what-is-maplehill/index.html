What Is Maplehill?

Maplehill is a once-thriving town and cultural center whose fading light and community the player helps revive. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

Maplehill is a once-thriving town and cultural center whose fading light and community the player helps revive.

Confirmed details

  • Its crisis is community decline.
  • It is culturally distinct from Tidewind and Quarrytop.
  • The player stays there during the journey.
