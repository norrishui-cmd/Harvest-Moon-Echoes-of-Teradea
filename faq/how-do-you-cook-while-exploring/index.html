How Do You Cook While Exploring Teradea?

Natsume confirms that meals can be cooked by the fire at campsites while traveling through the open world. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

Natsume confirms that meals can be cooked by the fire at campsites while traveling through the open world.

Confirmed details

  • Cooking is tied to campfires.
  • Camps also restore stamina.
