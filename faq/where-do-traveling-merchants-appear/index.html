Where Do Traveling Merchants Appear?

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and offer exclusive items that cannot be found elsewhere. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

Traveling merchants appear at campsites and offer exclusive items that cannot be found elsewhere.

Confirmed details

  • Campsites are the confirmed merchant location type.
  • The items are described as exclusive.
  • Merchant names and routes have not been announced.

What this means for players

Launch verification is still needed for campsite schedules, arrival triggers, inventory, prices, and restocking.
