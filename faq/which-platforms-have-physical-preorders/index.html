Which Platforms Have Physical Preorders?

Physical preorders are confirmed for Nintendo Switch 2, Nintendo Switch, and PlayStation 5. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

Physical preorders are confirmed for Nintendo Switch 2, Nintendo Switch, and PlayStation 5.

Confirmed details

  • Those three versions are named at Amazon and Natsume Store.
  • Xbox Series X|S and Steam remain launch platforms.
  • The announcement does not list physical Xbox or PC editions.

What this means for players

Digital preorder schedules and any later physical editions require separate confirmation.
