Is Echoes of Teradea Open World?

Yes. Natsume describes Echoes of Teradea as a massive open-world experience with villages, wilderness, maze-like caves and remote islands. See the confirmed details and the information still awaiting gameplay verification.

Quick answer

Yes. Natsume describes Echoes of Teradea as a massive open-world experience with villages, wilderness, maze-like caves and remote islands.

Confirmed facts

  • The world includes multiple named villages.
  • Caves contain ore and gems.
  • Nautical charts lead to remote islands.
  • Animal abilities and expanded movement open hidden routes.
