Are There Rare Animals in Echoes of Teradea?

Yes. Remote islands contain rare animals that are not found on the mainland, and players can befriend them. Confirmed details, player implications and the information still awaiting launch verification.

Quick answer

Yes. Remote islands contain rare animals that are not found on the mainland, and players can befriend them.
