What Can You Do at Campsites?

At campsites, players can rest, recover stamina, cook meals by the fire, and meet traveling merchants. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

At campsites, players can rest, recover stamina, cook meals by the fire, and meet traveling merchants.

Confirmed details

  • Campsites support recovery during long exploration trips.
  • Cooking is an explicit campfire function.
  • Merchants may carry exclusive items.

What this means for players

Exact camp locations, rest values, recipes, merchant schedules, and time costs have not been disclosed.

This answer is intentionally limited to the current game’s published information. It does not import values, characters, schedules, or mechanics from earlier Harvest Moon titles.
