What Is the Main Story Goal?

The broad goal is to help Teradea flourish again by supporting Guardian Spirits and villages, investigating disasters, and seeking a way to lift the mist. Confirmed facts, player context, and details still awaiting official or launch verification.

Quick answer

The broad goal is to help Teradea flourish again by supporting Guardian Spirits and villages, investigating disasters, and seeking a way to lift the mist.

Confirmed details

  • The mist covers Teradea.
  • Tidewind, Quarrytop, and Maplehill each face a distinct crisis.
  • Friendship and seasonal progress reveal more of the truth.

What this means for players

The full main-quest order, ending, and post-game state have not been revealed.
