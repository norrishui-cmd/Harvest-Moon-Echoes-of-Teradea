What Are Power Wisps in Echoes of Teradea?

Power Wisps are dormant beings freed by completing Power Statue challenges. The resulting Power Wisp Fruits can be exchanged for stamina and useful abilities. See the confirmed details and the information still awaiting gameplay verification.

Quick answer

Power Wisps are dormant beings freed by completing Power Statue challenges. The resulting Power Wisp Fruits can be exchanged for stamina and useful abilities.

Confirmed facts

  • Power Statues contain short challenges or puzzles.
  • Completing them frees Power Wisps.
  • The reward includes Power Wisp Fruits.
  • Fruits are traded at the Forest Goddess Statue.
