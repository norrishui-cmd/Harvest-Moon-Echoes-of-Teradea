Bloomfield Village in Harvest Moon: Echoes of Teradea

Confirmed story role of Bloomfield Village, the player character’s home village in Echoes of Teradea.

Quick answer

Bloomfield Village is the quiet village where the player character was raised. The journey begins as mist spreads from the nearby Forest of Echoes and wild wolves appear at night.

Confirmed details

  • Player character’s home village
  • Located near the Forest of Echoes
  • Connected to the opening wolf and mist storyline
  • A best friend joins the early mission
  • Full shops, residents and facilities are not yet confirmed
  • Launch guide will map every service and schedule

What this means for players

Bloomfield is a confirmed named location, but the available official material does not yet establish its full map, businesses or residents. Future additions must be based on screenshots, official previews or direct gameplay rather than assumptions from earlier Harvest Moon games.

Still to verify
