How the Player Restores Teradea

The player helps Guardian Spirits, assists villagers, investigates regional disasters and earns Happilia by contributing to Teradea’s development. These systems collectively support the world-restoration journey. Confirmed details, player implications and the information still awaiting launch verification.

Quick answer

The player helps Guardian Spirits, assists villagers, investigates regional disasters and earns Happilia by contributing to Teradea’s development. These systems collectively support the world-restoration journey.

Confirmed facts

  • Guardian Spirits are part of restoring Teradea.
  • Helping villagers earns Happilia.
  • Each named settlement has a regional problem.
  • Friendships and seasonal progression reveal the mist mystery.

What this means for players

This overview connects story and progression without inventing a single best restoration order. A step-by-step route requires actual quest prerequisites, resource costs, travel gates and rewards.

Still to verify
