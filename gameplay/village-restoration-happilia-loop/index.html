Village Restoration and Happilia Gameplay Loop

Players earn Happilia by helping villagers and contributing to Teradea’s development while investigating each settlement’s crisis and rebuilding the wider world. Current confirmed data, player implications, and remaining verification gaps.

Current answer

Players earn Happilia by helping villagers and contributing to Teradea’s development while investigating each settlement’s crisis and rebuilding the wider world.

Confirmed data

  • Tidewind faces storms.
  • Quarrytop faces earthquakes.
  • Maplehill needs community revival.
  • Helping development earns Happilia.

What this means for players

This creates a regional progression loop: discover a village problem, help residents, contribute to development, and advance Teradea’s restoration. Exact scoring should not be invented before gameplay.

Still awaiting verification

Whether Happilia is spent, global or village-specific, plus thresholds, rewards, and efficient sources remain unknown.
