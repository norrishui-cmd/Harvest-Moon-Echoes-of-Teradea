Campsite Day-Trip Gameplay Loop

Because Teradea is too large to explore in one day, campsites support longer trips through resting, stamina recovery, campfire cooking, and encounters with traveling merchants. Current confirmed data, player implications, and remaining verification gaps.

Current answer

Because Teradea is too large to explore in one day, campsites support longer trips through resting, stamina recovery, campfire cooking, and encounters with traveling merchants.

Confirmed data

  • Resting at camp recovers stamina.
  • Meals can be cooked by the fire.
  • Traveling merchants appear at campsites with exclusive goods.

What this means for players

Campsites link time management, stamina, food preparation, shopping, and route planning. Their value depends on location and schedule data that will need verification at launch.

Still awaiting verification

Camp locations, setup rules, time passage, recovery values, recipes, merchant schedules, and fast-travel behavior remain unknown.
