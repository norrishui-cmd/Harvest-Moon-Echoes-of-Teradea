Confirmed Public Details

What players know so far

Natsume has confirmed Harvest Moon: Echoes of Teradea as an open-world farming adventure releasing September 24, 2026. Official descriptions confirm a mysterious mist, wild wolves, threatened villages, Guardian Spirits, animal companions with traversal abilities, campsites, Power Statue challenges, ten romance options, farming and village restoration.

This site separates confirmed information from launch-day guide data. Crop profits, full maps, gift tables and quest steps are excluded from the sitemap until official information or verified gameplay can support a concrete answer.

FAQ

Common player questions

Is this an official Harvest Moon site?

No. This is an unofficial English fan guide concept and content hub. The hero image is original fan-site art, not official game art.

Is the release date final?

Natsume has officially confirmed September 24, 2026 for Switch, Switch 2, PS5, Xbox Series X|S and Steam.

Why are some guide details written as templates?

The game is unreleased as of July 15, 2026. Exact crop prices, romance gifts, maps, and quest steps should be filled after verified gameplay data exists.

What should be published first after launch?

First-week route, beginner mistakes, best early crops, tool upgrades, village restoration order, romance gifts, and map unlocks will likely produce the fastest value for players.
