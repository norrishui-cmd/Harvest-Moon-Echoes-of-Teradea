Characters and Villagers Hub

Character searches will grow after trailers and launch. This page establishes the correct structure now without inventing NPCs or romance candidates.

Character data status

No character names, schedules, favorite gifts, or romance candidate lists should be treated as confirmed unless they come from official material or verified gameplay.

Villager page template

  • Name, village or region, role, shop or service, and unlock condition.
  • Daily schedule by weather, season, festival day, and story progress if the game uses those variables.
  • Gift preferences with verification date and item links.
  • Quests, restoration requirements, relationship events, and romance status if applicable.

Internal cluster plan

Every character page should link to the romance hub, item database, village restoration hub, and relevant map region. That turns character content into a real guide system instead of a thin cast list.
