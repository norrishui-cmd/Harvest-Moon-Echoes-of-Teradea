Echoes of Teradea Characters

Confirmed Echoes of Teradea characters, allies and romance information, with unverified gifts and schedules excluded.

What is confirmed

Natsume has named the Harvest Goddess, Doc Jr. and Lupo, and has confirmed 10 romance candidates. Complete resident, gift and schedule data is not yet public.

Each linked page gives a direct answer and separates official facts from details that still require launch verification.

Harvest Goddess in Echoes of Teradea

The Harvest Goddess is a confirmed ally who guides the player during the effort to restore Teradea. Official material also names a Forest Goddess Statue used to exchange Power Wisp Fruits, but it does not yet clarify whether that statue represents the same character.

Doc Jr. in Harvest Moon: Echoes of Teradea

Doc Jr. is officially confirmed to return in Echoes of Teradea as an ingenious inventor and ally. Natsume has not yet detailed his inventions, home village, shop or quest chain in this game.

Who Is Lupo in Echoes of Teradea?

Lupo is identified by Natsume as the Bloomfield Guardian and appears as the baby wolf plush offered with eligible preorders. Official material reviewed so far does not fully explain Lupo’s in-game role, abilities or relationship to the rumored guardian wolf.

Romance Candidates

Ten love interests are confirmed: five bachelors and five bachelorettes.
