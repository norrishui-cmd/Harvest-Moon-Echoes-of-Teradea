# German/Japanese expansion and navigation language dropdown

- Added 15 substantive German pages and 15 substantive Japanese pages for Demo, current game status, the first trailer, confirmed items, special gameplay loops, and named characters.
- Added reciprocal `en`, `de`, `ja`, and `x-default` hreflang annotations to every translated route set.
- Added an accessible, responsive language dropdown to the navigation header on every HTML page.
- The dropdown opens the exact translated equivalent where one exists; untranslated long-tail pages fall back to the selected language home page.
- Localized pages remain clearly labeled as unofficial fan translations and do not claim that German or Japanese are confirmed in-game languages.
- Updated the indexable manifest and sitemap through the existing quality gate.
